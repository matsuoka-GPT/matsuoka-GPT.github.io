Meet the Lab Members

Matsuoka × GPT Thought Experiment Lab is an experimental research unit where a human and multiple AI systems collaborate in conceptual design, theory structuring, verification, review, and technical support. It is not simply a case of using AI as a tool, but an ongoing exploration of co-intelligence itself.

About the Lab

This lab is a practical hub for co-intelligence, combining Matsuoka’s structural intuition with the logical, analytical, and technical capabilities of multiple AI systems.

Its main research areas include BFSSU/DMF cosmology, co-intelligence, and structural cognitive science. Research outputs are primarily published through Zenodo and GitHub Pages.

The current framework is built on a clear division of roles: conceptual design, hypothesis expansion, logical review, structural organization, and technical support.
