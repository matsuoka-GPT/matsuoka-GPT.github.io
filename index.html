Research Profile
Takafumi Matsuoka is an independent researcher and concept architect working on interdisciplinary structural theory across physics, complex systems, and socio-technical systems.
His primary research includes the BFSSU/DMF cosmological model (Black Hole Fractal Steady-State Universe / Dynamic Mass Flow), ontology of physical entities (photon ontology and quantum vacuum structure), and structural analysis of intelligence and institutional stability (co-intelligence and assurance-based governance models).
His work focuses on reconstructing scientific and social frameworks through formalized structural analysis and reproducible thought experiments.
This website functions as a public research hub.
The latest publications and citation information are maintained via ORCID and Zenodo.

方法論（How we work）

  1. 仮説：構造的必然・再現性を重視して定義
  2. 照合：既知データ・既存理論・反例の探索（矛盾があれば修正/棄却）
  3. 論文化：第三者が追える形（前提・限界・予測）に落とす
  4. 公開：DOI付与・多言語化・外部からの質問対応
